Title: Why I'm Not Just a Developer Anymore

Hey there.

I'm a creative technologist and full-stack content maker with roots in front-end development and a brain wired for storytelling, video editing, and experimentation. A Full-Stack Full Stack Content Creator.

After 15 years building UI systems, tools, and dashboards in the tech world, I shifted into full-time content creation. These days I work across everything from video production and music editing to automation, livestreaming, and investigative media. My favorite projects combine creativity, tech, and emotion. Something weird. Something memorable. Something that works.

I've led long-term dev projects inside SaaS companies, managed live content feeds, scored and edited music videos, built interactive experiences, and trained bots to behave like cartoon squirrels. I don't stick to one lane. I follow what feels alive.

Recently I realized I don't love coding. I don't love editing. I don't love music, journalism, or livestreaming for their own sake. What I love is making things that people can actually see. I care about putting love, passion, and a little luck into everything I create, and giving people something that makes them feel it too.

I don't want to influence. I want to inspire.

Based in Cincinnati. Open to remote-friendly roles globally across any timezone.
Available for freelance, contract, and full-time work.
Always down to collaborate on something that shouldn't exist but somehow works.
